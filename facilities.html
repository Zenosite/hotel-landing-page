Facilities
Swimming Pool

Vhotel offers a variety of facilities, including a spacious swimming pool of 100m2. The pool is accompanied by a beautiful garden, providing a serene environment for guests to unwind and make the most of their leisure time. Moreover, our swimming pool incorporates salt-chlorination technology, ensuring a gentle experience for the eyes and skin. Additionally, we have a separate children's pool available, and for those looking to dine, our Poolside Restaurant conveniently adjoins the outdoor pool, creating a delightful dining experience.

Gym

Welcome to VhoTel Gym, a premier fitness facility that offers complete equipment to cater to all your workout needs. Our gym is equipped with cardiovascular machines, strength training equipment, and a functional training area, allowing you to engage in a wide range of exercises. Whether you prefer cardio workouts, weightlifting, or functional training, we have you covered. What sets us apart is our commitment to accessibility - our gym is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, ensuring that you can exercise at your convenience, anytime. Join us and experience the ultimate fitness journey in a welcoming and well-equipped environment.

Spa

Indulge in the ultimate relaxation and rejuvenation experience at VhoTel Spa, where our team of highly skilled professionals is dedicated to providing you with the best time of your life. Our spa offers a diverse range of treatments designed to release stress, relieve tension, and leave you feeling completely at ease. From soothing massages to luxurious facials and pampering spa packages. Step into our serene oasis and let our experts guide you towards a state of blissful tranquility, ensuring an unforgettable spa experience that will leave you feeling refreshed and stress-free.

Restaurant

Welcome to VhoTel Restaurant, a culinary destination renowned for its top-class dining experience. Our restaurant boasts a team of exceptional chefs who skillfully prepare your meals using the finest, freshest ingredients available. We take pride in offering an incredible range of delectable dishes, carefully curated to satisfy diverse palates and dietary preferences. Whether you crave international cuisine, local specialties, or innovative fusion creations, our menu offers a plethora of options to delight your taste buds. Immerse yourself in a world of culinary excellence as you savor the exceptional flavors and impeccable presentation that define our restaurant.
