Ancol Dreamland 9.6 KM
06:00 - 22:00 WIB
Ancol Dreamland, is the largest and most renowned recreational park in Jakarta, featuring several attractions such as Ancol Bay, Fantasy World Dufan, SeaWorld, Atlantis Water Adventure, Ocean Dream Samudra, Pasar Seni Art Market, and Ancol Golf.
Nasional Gallery 5.2 KM
10:00 - 16:00 WIB
The National Gallery of Indonesia provides valuable insights into the art history and development of the archipelago through its extensive collection, encompassing significant archives and contemporary art exhibitions both from Indonesia and internationally.
Plaza Indonesia 0.1 KM
10:00 - 22:00 WIB
Plaza Indonesia, renowned as the foremost luxury shopping destination in the country, features an extensive range of international brands spanning fashion, food, and furniture on its ground and first floors, offering a comprehensive selection that ensures customer satisfaction.
Grand Indonesia 0.2 KM
10:00 - 22:00 WIB
Grand Indonesia is appropriately referred to as a shopping town due to its interconnected buildings spanning eight stories, offering a wide range of dining and retail options on every floor, alongside the Galeri Indonesia Kaya, providing experience to learn Indonesian culture.
